About the Project

Research for "Decoding the Narrator: Machine Learning Approaches to Audiobook Performance Analysis" was conducted in Summer 2024 as part of the Anderson Science Fellows program. The project focused on implementing, synthesizing, and expanding upon existing machine learning methods for audio data featurization and classification, with the primary objective of conducting computational analysis of audiobook narrators. The research team focused on classifying different aspects of audiobook narrators, such as narrator identity, demographic attributes (age, gender, etc.), style of delivery (flat affect, impressions of character voices, etc.).
